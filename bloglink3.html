Positive Impact of Technology on Footwear

We don’t usually spend much time thinking about what’s on our feet. We decide what to wear, go through the door and carry on with our plans. Yet in the background, there has been an important shift that is not widely known. Modern technology is giving shoes a big update and now the making process is almost unbelievable. So, designers rely on computer tools to develop and evaluate their ideas without adding a single material. Manufactured shoes are produced cleaner, with less waste and end up being better than the handmade ones. It feels as if we’ve given the traditional realm of shoe-creation a fresh, modern edge.The shopping side also has been transformed. Have you ever taken a photo of your feet in potential shoes from home? This has turned into a real thing — and it’s effective too. Other shoes include small sensors to help runners just like having your own fitness trainer inside your pair. Thanks to technology, brands are able to hear what we like, how we move and what we care about. After all, being smarter about shoes isn’t the only benefit. It’s centered on shoes that are right for us, go where we go and feel right.
