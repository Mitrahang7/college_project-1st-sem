Impact of Web Development in E-Commerce

Web development is a powerful driving force behind the success of modern e-commerce. As online shopping continues to grow, a well-developed website determines how users interact with and trust a brand. From layout and responsiveness to loading speed and secure payments, every detail of a site influences customer decisions.

Responsive web design ensures that users can access the platform from desktops, tablets, and smartphones without issues. A user-friendly interface with organized product listings, clear navigation, fast checkout processes, and engaging visuals helps improve customer satisfaction and increases sales.

Moreover, features like search filters, product recommendations, secure payment gateways, reviews, and multilingual support are made possible through solid web development practices. These enhance the shopping experience and encourage customers to return.

In competitive markets like footwear, websites must reflect current fashion trends, offer diverse products, and stand out with excellent design and functionality. This research explores how Nepali e-commerce platforms use web development strategies and tools to deliver engaging, responsive, and reliable user experiences.

Ekjor.com

Walk Style

Walkstyle shoes is a Nepali e-commerce online shopping platform particularly targeting to collage stydents. This platform focuses to meet the needs of students by offering a diverse range footware options at suitable prices.

Walkstyle gives selection of footware with competitive price according to the budgets with exclusive discounts on students. Caliber

Caliber shoes is a kathmandu based footware brand which provide quality, style and affordability established in 2015 by Aman and Prashanna Gautam. It is well designed shoes to prevent the low quality import in the local market.

Caliber shoes gives wide variety of footware to match in different preferences and occasions. Daraz

Daraz is the leading and popular e-commerical platforms that connects buyers with multiple of local and international sellers.
