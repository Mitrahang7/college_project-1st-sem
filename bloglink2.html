Impact of Technology on Footwear Business

Innovation has become a bigger focus in the footwear business than just leather, laces and soles. Technology is changing the manufacture, sale and style of shoes. By using 3D printing, companies are able to make shoes that fit perfectly and cause fewer production problems. As a result, fewer shoes end up in landfills and buyers are happy to shop for shoes without leaving their homes. Because of augmented reality (AR) and virtual try-ons, viewers can see on their screens how a pair of shoes would look when worn. People end up feeling safe using online shops more often and businesses love it too, since it means fewer returns and happier customers.Technology quietly helps companies be more effective in the background. By looking at data, we are able to see which styles are popular and what people like to buy. Logistics companies use sophisticated software to manage stock, ensuring neither storage space nor the shelves are empty or full.
